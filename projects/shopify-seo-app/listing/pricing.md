# SEOPilot — Pricing Details

## Free Plan
- **Price**: $0/month
- **AI Credits**: 10/month
- **Features**:
  - Basic SEO scan (all resource types)
  - SEO health score dashboard
  - 10 AI fix credits per month
  - Manual fix application
  - Fix history and rollback

## Pro Plan (Recommended)
- **Price**: $19/month
- **Trial**: 7-day free trial
- **AI Credits**: 100/month
- **Features**:
  - Everything in Free
  - Full SEO scan with advanced rules
  - 100 AI fix credits per month
  - Batch fix operations
  - Priority scan scheduling
  - Email notifications

## Business Plan
- **Price**: $39/month
- **AI Credits**: Unlimited
- **Features**:
  - Everything in Pro
  - Unlimited AI fix credits
  - Priority support
  - Custom AI tone settings
  - API access (coming soon)
  - Multi-store support (coming soon)

## Billing Details
- All plans are billed monthly through Shopify
- Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time
- AI credits reset at the start of each billing cycle
- Unused credits do not roll over
- Cancel anytime from your Shopify admin
