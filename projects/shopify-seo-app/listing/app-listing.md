## Long Description

### Boost Your Store's Search Rankings with AI

SEOPilot is your all-in-one SEO optimization tool for Shopify. It scans your entire store — products, collections, and pages — identifies SEO issues, and uses AI to generate optimized content in seconds.

**Stop losing sales to poor SEO.** Most Shopify stores have critical SEO issues they don't even know about. SEOPilot finds them and fixes them automatically.

### Key Features

**Comprehensive SEO Scanner**
- Scan all products, collections, and pages in one click
- Get a 0-100 SEO health score for your entire store
- Identify issues: missing meta titles, short descriptions, missing alt text, duplicate content, poor URL slugs, and more
- Track your SEO progress over time

**AI-Powered Auto-Fix**
- Generate optimized meta titles (30-60 characters, keyword-rich)
- Create compelling meta descriptions (120-160 characters)
- Write descriptive image alt text for accessibility and SEO
- Enhance product descriptions with SEO-friendly copy
- Choose from Professional, Casual, or Luxury writing tones

**Safe & Reversible**
- Preview every AI suggestion before applying
- Full fix history with one-click revert
- Batch operations for fixing multiple issues at once
- Never lose your original content

**Smart Dashboard**
- Real-time SEO health score
- Issue breakdown by type and severity
- Score distribution across all resources
- Quick actions to scan and fix

5. **Apply** — Preview and apply fixes to your store
